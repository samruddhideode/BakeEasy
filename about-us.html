ABOUT US

Meet the founders of Bake For Change

Mrs. Rupali Deode has been an avid cook and baker all her life. She has been giving cooking classes ranging from healthy salads and various continental cuisines to fresh cream cake baking and decoration. Samruddhi has grown up watching her mom bake but never took up baking till the pandemic struck and left her with loads of free time to try her hand at cake decorating. During the lockdown and the subsequent months that followed Samruddhi realised that people were looking for hygienic home cooked items from trusted sources. While Rupali often baked cakes for friends and family, Samruddhi decided to take a step ahead and started Bake for Change to cater to the increasing demand. She loves baking as a hobby and never meant to earn an income from it. So combining with their common love for baking and strong feelings for those who suffered the worst during the pandemic, the mother-daughter duo decided that a part of the proceeds from Bake for Change will go to the needy. Till today, Rupali and Samruddhi have provided about 50 families with hot meals with the money raised from Bake for Change since its inception in May 2020.
